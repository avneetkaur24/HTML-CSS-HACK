About Us

Located in the serene hills of Himachal Pradesh, NavGurukul's Himachal Campus is a unique initiative focusing on self-study and peer-learning for BCA students. Our campus promotes independence, critical thinking, and collaborative learning in a peaceful environment.Navgurukul is a non-profit organization in India that focuses on providing accessible and quality education in software development to underprivileged and marginalized youth, particularly those from low-income families. The organization aims to equip students with the skills needed to secure employment in the tech industry, thereby empowering them to improve their socio-economic status.

Our Partners

Eternal University

Eternal University partners with Navgurukul to provide an accredited BCA degree to students. This partnership ensures that the curriculum meets the educational standards required for a formal degree while integrating the practical, hands-on training provided by Navgurukul.

Eternal University is a private university established under the Himachal Pradesh Government Act and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

SBA

Shri Badrika Ashram provides a peaceful and conducive environment for learning. The partnership with Navgurukul allows students to stay at the Ashram during their education, benefiting from its tranquil setting. The Ashram also offers spiritual guidance and moral support, helping students develop a strong ethical foundation alongside their technical education.The Ashram's involvement emphasizes the importance of holistic development, blending technical education with spiritual growth, moral values, and community living. This unique approach aims to create well-rounded individuals who are not only skilled in their profession but also grounded in ethics and social responsibility.

SIGNIFICANCE OF PARTNERSHIP

The collaboration between Navgurukul, Eternal University, and Shri Badrika Ashram creates a unique educational model that combines academic excellence, practical skill development, and spiritual growth. This partnership ensures that students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers while also nurturing their personal and ethical development.
